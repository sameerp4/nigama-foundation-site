Why Merchandise?

A Donation You Can Put On.

Most giving is invisible. A t-shirt isn't. Every item in this collection turns a supporter into a walking introduction — a conversation that starts with "what's Nigama Foundation?" and ends with one more person who knows the work.

And unlike a logo giveaway, this is real funding: half of every rupee you spend goes straight into a program you chose yourself.

Visibility

Every shirt worn to the gym, every notebook on an office desk carries the foundation's name into rooms we could never reach on our own.

Community

Merchandise marks belonging. Volunteers, donors, and staff wearing the same crest is a quiet statement: this mission has people behind it.

Funding

The 50% split is not a rounding gesture. A single jacket funds ₹1,250 of program work — a month of school supplies for two children, delivered in person.

Questions

Before You Order

How does shipping work?

We ship across India within 7–10 business days of order confirmation. Shipping within Hyderabad, Vizag, and Chennai is free; a flat ₹80 applies elsewhere in India. Once we confirm your order by email, we'll share tracking details as soon as your item is dispatched.

How do I pick the right size?

Apparel runs true to standard Indian sizing (S through XXL). When we email your order confirmation, we'll include a size chart with chest and length measurements — you can confirm or adjust your size before anything ships. Exchanges for unworn items are free within 14 days.

How is the 50% contribution tracked?

Every order is logged against the program you selected, and contributions are transferred to that program's ledger monthly. Nigama Foundation is 12AB registered and 80G certified, and merchandise-driven contributions appear in our published impact reporting — you'll see exactly which drives and projects your program funded.
