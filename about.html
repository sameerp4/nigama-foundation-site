The Founder

From Staffing Rooms to School Rooms

Sameer Penakalapati has spent his career connecting people to opportunity. As CEO of Indotronix Avani Group, a US-India staffing firm, he's placed thousands of professionals into work that changed their trajectories. But staffing serves those already at the door. Nigama Foundation exists for those still far from it.

Founded in Hyderabad, the foundation carries his conviction that opportunity begins with dignity: a classroom with an auditorium, an elder with company, a student with a counselor who listens. The numbers on this site are that principle, delivered. Nigama Foundation serves as the philanthropic arm of both Indotronix Avani Group and Ceipal.

Our Mission

Why We Exist

We believe dignity isn't given. It's delivered in person, by hand, on time. Nigama Foundation works across Hyderabad, Vizag, and Chennai to deliver education to students who lack supplies and infrastructure, care to elders who've been left waiting, and respect to people with disabilities whom society too often looks past.

Three pillars. One promise. Every rupee arrives as something you can touch.

Delivery over declaration

We measure ourselves by what arrives, not what we announce.

Specificity over scale

One name, one classroom, one delivery at a time.

Consistency over campaigns

Quarterly drives, not one-time gestures.

Dignity as the standard

Every action is judged by whether it preserves the recipient's dignity.
