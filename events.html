Project Parivarthan

A Movement for Young Minds

2026
Kurnool District · 2026
June 2026 Youth Mental Health · Andhra Pradesh

Project Parivarthan: Setting the Tone for 2026

Expanded across 8 districts of Andhra Pradesh — reaching 170+ institutions and 22,000+ young adults with structured mental health awareness workshops. Screen addiction, peer pressure, emotional resilience: conversations India's classrooms rarely have, delivered by professionals who show up in person.

Phase 1 · Nov–Dec 2025 — 16 institutions across Kurnool & Nandyal, 3,500+ youth reached for the first time
Phase 2 · 2025–2026 — Expanded to 170+ institutions across 8 districts of Andhra Pradesh, 22,000+ youth
