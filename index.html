We believe dignity isn't given. It's delivered in person, by hand, on time. Nigama Foundation works across Hyderabad, Vizag, and Chennai to deliver education to students who lack supplies and infrastructure, care to elders who've been left waiting, and respect to people with disabilities whom society too often looks past.

In Their Words

Clean Water, Delivered.

In June 2025, Nigama Foundation installed a water filtration system at a government school in a village near Anantapur, Andhra Pradesh. For years, students had been drinking from an untreated borewell. The water looked clear. The effects were not.

The new filtration unit now delivers clean drinking water to over 300 students every school day. No more students going home with stomach infections. No more parents choosing to pull kids from school because they kept falling sick.

"The children used to complain of stomach pain almost every week. Since the filter was installed, we have not had a single case."

School Headmaster, Anantapur District
